Biography


Dr. Shaima is Freelancer Researcher and Data Scientist. She studied a Bachelor's in the Faculty of Computer Science (Major in Information Technology) at Cairo University 2008, Cairo City (FCI). After a tenure of One year as an assistant professor at Taiz university in Yemen. She was awarded a Master's degree in Information Computer Science at FCI Egypt in 2016. Afterward, She started his doctoral studies at Systems at Universitat Rovira I Virgili (URV) in Tarragona, Catalonia, Spain. She was becoming part of the "Sensorial Systems Applied to the Industry" (SSAI) research group, led by Prof. Francesc Serratosa.
Other relevant experiences include being a researcher at the Remote Sensing department in the Ministry of Oil Beside working as Method logistic engineer at Total-Yemen. She worked on different data science projects.
